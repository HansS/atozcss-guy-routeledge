Hi, I'm Guy Routledge and I'm the voice of AtoZ CSS.

I learned everything I know from reading blogs and watching screencasts. Now I'm sharing what I've learned so you can sharpen your skills and stay up to date in the fast-moving world of web development.

As a contract front-end developer, I have the opportunity to work with a wide variety of clients on a range of different projects. But I’ve been working closely with General Assembly teaching front-end development in London and have been loving every minute of it. It’s been a whole new set of challenges but incredibly rewarding — and has proved to me just how much I love what I do.

Classroom teaching is great, but in an effort to spread the front-end love far and wide, I’ve put together this site and these videos on my favourite topic: CSS. The first series of 26 videos tackles a different CSS topic, concept, selector, property or value — one for each letter of the alphabet.

CSS is what got me hooked on web development, which in turn has given me an incredible quality of life; the freedom to work on the projects I choose, a flexible schedule, the opportunity to build other businesses and invest in my future and the chance to share valueable knowledge through speaking, teaching and writing. My hope is that the resources here help you acheive your goals and aspirations too.

My goal with this site is to share knowledge and empower you to design, build and launch amazing projects for yourself, your employer or your clients.

If there's anything you need a hand with, just shoot me a tweet and ask away. If there's any topic you'd like to see covered in future videos, articles, books or courses please let me know.

Cheers.
