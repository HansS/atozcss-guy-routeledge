AtoZ Production Resources

If you've ever wanted to know about the tech and process behind the AtoZ screencasts then look no further.

I have a behind the scenes video and details of all the kit below.

Slides are designed and animated in Keynote — as was the opening sequence.

Audio is recorded with a Blue Yeti microphone and Samson pop filter mounted on an adjustable Microphone Boom Arm with a Radius shock mount (Amazon affiliate links).

Video is recorded at 1280x720 with Screenflow (affiliate link) which is also where audio and video are stitched together into the final episodes.

Videos are hosted on Wistia.

The Mailing List and emails are Powered by MailChimp (affiliate link).
